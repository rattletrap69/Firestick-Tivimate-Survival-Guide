Firestick Basics & Maintenance

Force Restart / Unfreeze

If the Firestick is completely frozen and you can't reach the power plug, use this remote shortcut:

Press and hold both the 🔘 Select button AND the ⏯️ Play/Pause button at the exact same time for 10 seconds. The screen will go black and the Firestick will reboot.

Checking for System Updates

A common reason for Firestick lag is pending Amazon updates. Force it to check:

  1. Go to the ⚙️ Gear Icon (Settings).
  2. Select My Fire TV > About.
  3. Scroll down and click 🔘 Select on Check for Updates or Install Update.

Uninstalling / Force Stopping Apps

If storage is full or an app is misbehaving:

  1. Go to ⚙️ Settings > Applications > Manage Installed Applications.
  2. Select the misbehaving or unused app.
  3. To fix a frozen app: Click 🔘 Select on Force Stop, then Launch.
  4. To delete an app: Click 🔘 Select on Uninstall to completely remove it.

Wi-Fi & Remote Pairing

Reconnect Wi-Fi: Go to ⚙️ Settings > Network. Select your network and enter the password.
Fix a Dead Remote: Swap AAA batteries. Stand near the TV, press and hold the 🏠 Home Button for 10 seconds to re-pair it.

TiviMate Basics

Blank TV Guide (No Info)

Sometimes the guide fails to download automatically. Force it to update:

  1. Inside TiviMate, press ⬅️ Left Directional until the side menu slides out.
  2. Scroll down to ⚙️ Settings (gear icon).
  3. Select EPG.
  4. Click 🔘 Select on Update EPG. Wait a minute for channels to fill.

Buffering or Freezing

Always check storage first (Clear Cache). If still buffering:

  1. In TiviMate, go to ⚙️ Settings > Playlists.
  2. Click your playlist name.
  3. Scroll down and click 🔘 Select on Update Playlist.
  4. If all fails: Unplug Firestick power, unplug Wi-Fi router, wait 60s, plug back in.

Setting Up "Favorites"

Stop scrolling through 1,000s of channels.

  1. Highlight a channel you watch often in the guide.
  2. Press and hold the 🔘 Select button for 2 seconds.
  3. A menu pops up on the right. Click Add to Favorites (a star will appear).
  4. To view: Press ⬅️ Left Directional to open the menu, scroll up to Favorites.

Auto-Start on Boot

Make TiviMate act like a normal cable box.

  1. Go to ⚙️ Settings > General.
  2. Turn Auto start on boot ON.
  3. Go back to ⚙️ Settings > EPG.
  4. Turn Update on app start ON (keeps guide fresh when turning on TV).

EPG Troubleshooting Deep-Dive

The "Nuke & Pave" Fix

If "Update EPG" doesn't work, your guide cache might be corrupted. Clear it entirely and start fresh:

  1. In TiviMate, go to ⚙️ Settings > EPG.
  2. Scroll to the very bottom and click 🔘 Select on Clear EPG.
  3. Once it says cleared, scroll up and click Update EPG.
  4. Wait 2-3 minutes for the fresh data to download.

Assigning a Missing Channel

If only *one* or *two* channels say "No Information", you can manually link them to the correct guide data:

  1. Highlight the broken channel in your TV Guide.
  2. Press and hold 🔘 Select for 2 seconds.
  3. Scroll down the right menu to Assign EPG.
  4. Click the search icon 🔍, type the name of the channel (e.g., "ESPN"), and select the correct match from the list.

Adding Backup EPG Sources

If your provider's TV guide is completely offline, you can add free, community-maintained EPGs (like EPGShare or Bevy) to fill in the blanks.

Part 1: Adding the Source

  1. Go to ⚙️ Settings > EPG > EPG Sources.
  2. Click Add source.
  3. Carefully type in a known community EPG URL.
    (Example: https://epgshare01.online/epgshare01/epg_ripper_US1.xml.gz)
  4. Click Next and let it process.

Part 2: Applying it to your Playlist

  1. Go to ⚙️ Settings > Playlists.
  2. Click on your playlist name.
  3. Scroll down to EPG Sources.
  4. Find the new URL you just added, and toggle it to ON.
  5. Go back and run Update EPG.

Community Fixes & Reddit Hacks

The "User-Agent" Trick

The Problem: You get a HTTP 401/403 Error, or your ISP (Internet Service Provider) is actively blocking/throttling your IPTV connection because they recognize the TiviMate app signature.

The Reddit Fix: Disguise TiviMate to look like a standard web browser or a different video player.

  1. Go to ⚙️ Settings > General.
  2. Scroll down to User-Agent and click it.
  3. Type exactly: IPTVSmartersPro or VLC
  4. Click Next/Done. This frequently bypasses basic ISP blocks.

Fix Black Screens & Audio Issues

The Problem: You click a channel, hear perfect audio, but the screen is completely black. Or, the video is extremely choppy/glitchy while audio is fine.

The Reddit Fix: The Firestick's hardware chip is failing to decode the specific video format. Switch the processing over to software.

  1. Go to ⚙️ Settings > Playback.
  2. Click on Audio/Video Decoder.
  3. Change it from Hardware (HW) to Software (SW).
  4. Note: You can also toggle this on a per-channel basis using the bottom quick menu while watching a show.

The External Player VOD Hack

The Problem: You are trying to watch a VOD (Video on Demand) Movie, but it keeps crashing, has no sound, or doesn't have the subtitle languages you need.

The Reddit Fix: Use TiviMate as a library, but offload the actual playing of the movie to a dedicated media player app (like VLC or MX Player) which handles massive video files much better.

  1. Install VLC for Fire from the Amazon App Store.
  2. In TiviMate, go to your Movie/VOD section and highlight the movie.
  3. Press and hold 🔘 Select.
  4. Click Open in external player and select VLC.

Beating ISP Throttling (VPNs)

The Problem: The TV guide works perfectly all day, but channels start aggressively buffering specifically between 6:00 PM and 10:00 PM, or during big sporting events.

The Reddit Fix: Your internet provider (Comcast, Spectrum, etc.) is likely throttling heavy IPTV traffic during peak hours.

  • If the User-Agent trick (above) fails, the only guaranteed community fix is to use a VPN (Virtual Private Network).
  • Install an app like Surfshark, NordVPN, or ExpressVPN directly onto the Firestick.
  • Connect to a server (usually your closest major city). This scrambles your data, preventing your ISP from knowing you are streaming IPTV, bypassing the throttle.

TiviMate Advanced Features

Catch-Up (Past Shows)

Watch shows that aired yesterday (if supported by provider).

  • Look for a small 🕒 Clock Icon next to the channel name in the guide.
  • If present, simply press and hold ⬅️ Left to scroll back in time to past programs.
  • Click 🔘 Select on a past show to watch it immediately.

Closed Captions (CC)

Enable subtitles for Live TV or VOD Movies.

  • While watching, press 🔘 Select to open the bottom menu.
  • Scroll right to CC and select a subtitle track.
  • VOD Movie Fix: Use the "External Player Hack" (VLC) above to download subtitles online.

Fix Audio Sync

If the lips don't match the sound on a channel:

  • While watching, press 🔘 Select to bring up the bottom menu.
  • Scroll over to the Audio Sync icon.
  • Adjust the offset up or down in milliseconds until the audio matches the video perfectly.

Multi-Screen

Watch up to 4 channels at once (Requires multiple provider connections).

  • While watching TV, press 🔘 Select for the bottom menu.
  • Scroll to Multi-screen.
  • Select Add Screen and choose another channel.
  • Use directional arrows to select which screen plays audio.

Clean Up Channel Names

Remove annoying prefixes like "US | " from channel names.

  • Go to ⚙️ Settings > Appearance > TV Guide.
  • Click Channel Names Editor and turn it ON.
  • Under Prefixes to remove, type exactly what you want hidden (e.g., US, UK, CA, FHD).

Backup / Restore

Save all your favorites and settings before making big changes.

  • Go to ⚙️ Settings > General.
  • Scroll to the bottom and click Backup Data.
  • Select a local folder (or USB Drive) to save the file.
  • To load a backup, click Restore Data and select the file.

Recording on Firestick

Hardware Requirements

Firesticks have very little internal storage. To record TV successfully, you must add external storage.

  • OTG Cable / Adapter: Plugs into your Firestick to split the power port so you can connect a USB drive.
  • USB Flash Drive: Must be formatted to FAT32. (Note: FAT32 limits individual files to 4GB, which equals roughly 1-2 hours of HD recording).

1. Configure TiviMate Recording Folder

  1. In TiviMate, go to ⚙️ Settings > Other > Recording.
  2. Click 🔘 Select on Recordings Folder.
  3. Use the System Picker to locate your attached USB Flash Drive.
  4. Select the folder (or create one named 'Recordings') and grant TiviMate permissions.

Recording Live TV (Right Now)

  1. While watching a program, press the 🔘 Select button to bring up the lower menu.
  2. Use ➡️ Right Directional to scroll to the 🔴 Record icon.
  3. Click 🔘 Select to start recording.

Scheduling a Recording (Future)

  1. In the TV Guide, highlight the future program.
  2. Press and hold the 🔘 Select button to open the right-side menu.
  3. Scroll to Custom Recording and click 🔘 Select.
  4. Verify the times and optionally toggle Repeat for series recording.
