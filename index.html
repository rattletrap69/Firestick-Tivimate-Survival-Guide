Clearing Out Space

We need to delete temporary files to make room for your TV guide.

CRITICAL: Do NOT click "Clear Data". Only click "Clear Cache".

  1. Press the 🏠 Home Button on your remote.
  2. Scroll all the way to the right using the ⏺️ Directional Circle to the ⚙️ Gear Icon (Settings).

Recording on Firestick

Hardware Requirements

Firesticks have very little internal storage. To record TV successfully, you must add external storage.

  • OTG Cable / Adapter: Plugs into your Firestick to split the power port so you can connect a USB drive.
  • USB Flash Drive: Must be formatted to FAT32. (Note: FAT32 limits individual files to 4GB, which equals roughly 1-2 hours of HD recording).

1. Configure TiviMate Recording Folder

  1. In TiviMate, go to ⚙️ Settings > Other > Recording.
  2. Click 🔘 Select on Recordings Folder.
  3. Use the System Picker to locate your attached USB Flash Drive.
  4. Select the folder (or create one named 'Recordings') and grant TiviMate permissions.

Recording Live TV (Right Now)

  1. While watching a program, press the 🔘 Select button to bring up the lower menu.
  2. Use ➡️ Right Directional to scroll to the 🔴 Record icon.
  3. Click 🔘 Select to start recording.

Scheduling a Recording (Future)

  1. In the TV Guide, highlight the future program.
  2. Press and hold the 🔘 Select button to open the right-side menu.
  3. Scroll to Custom Recording and click 🔘 Select.
  4. Verify the times and optionally toggle Repeat for series recording.

TiviMate Optimization & Fixes

Blank TV Guide (No Info)

Sometimes the guide fails to download automatically. Force it to update:

  1. Inside TiviMate, press ⬅️ Left Directional until the side menu slides out.
  2. Scroll down to ⚙️ Settings (gear icon).
  3. Select EPG.
  4. Click 🔘 Select on Update EPG. Wait a minute for channels to fill.

Buffering or Freezing

Always check storage first (Clear Cache). If still buffering:

  1. In TiviMate, go to ⚙️ Settings > Playlists.
  2. Click your playlist name (pl9245637).
  3. Scroll down and click 🔘 Select on Update Playlist.
  4. If all fails: Unplug Firestick power, unplug Wi-Fi router, wait 60s, plug back in.

Setting Up "Favorites"

Stop scrolling through 1,000s of channels.

  1. Highlight a channel you watch often.
  2. Press and hold the 🔘 Select button for 2s until the right menu pops up.
  3. Click Add to Favorites (a star will appear).
  4. To view them: Press ⬅️ Left Directional to open the menu, scroll up to Favorites.

Auto-Start on Boot

Make TiviMate act like a normal cable box.

  1. Go to ⚙️ Settings > General.
  2. Turn Auto start on boot ON.
  3. Go back to ⚙️ Settings > EPG.
  4. Turn Update on app start ON (keeps guide fresh).

Default TiviMate Remote Map

🔘 Select / Center Button

  • Quick press (Live TV): Brings up bottom Quick Menu & History.
  • Long press (Live TV): Opens the Right-side context menu (Add to Fav, Assign EPG).

↩️ Back Button

  • Quick press (Live TV): Returns to TV Guide.
  • Long press (In Guide): Returns instantly to currently playing program.

⬅️ Left Directional

  • Quick press: Opens Group/Playlist side-menu.
  • Long press (Guide): Scrolls to Past Programs (Catch-up).

➡️ Right Directional

  • Quick press (Live): Jumps to previously watched channel.
